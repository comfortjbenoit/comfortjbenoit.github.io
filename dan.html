Fill Existing PDF Form

Fill the form below and click “Fill & Download PDF” or “Fill & Preview”. The script populates fields in the embedded PDF form:

Store Number → str_num
Employee Name → emp_name
Date of Disciplinary Action → dan_date
Job Title → emp_job (dropdown in PDF)
Company Policy Violated + Date of Policy Infraction + policyDescriptions → txt_sect1
Disciplinary Action Taken → txt_sect2

Will be converted to Proper Case when pasted to PDF.
